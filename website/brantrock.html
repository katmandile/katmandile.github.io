Brant Rock

Brant Rock is located in Ocean Bluffs in Marshfield. Driving along Brant Rock you will come up to the Sea Wall, which is a big man-made wall to prevent waves from causing any damage to houses. The Sea Wall is by far a favorite attraction of tourists. It's the perfect place to eat breakfast, read a book, or just to sit and think. At nighttime Brant Rock as a variety of bars and restaurants to choose from including, Haddads, The Jetty, and The Venus.
